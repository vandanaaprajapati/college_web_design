Intermediate

Stream Selection: In Indian intermediate education, students typically choose from three main streams: Science, Commerce, and Arts (Humanities). The choice of stream determines the subjects a student will study in depth during these two years. Science Stream: This stream includes subjects like Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics/Biology, and sometimes Computer Science. Students opting for this stream often pursue careers in engineering, medicine, research, or related fields. Commerce Stream: Commerce stream includes subjects like Accountancy, Business Studies, Economics, and Mathematics. Students in this stream often aim for careers in finance, accounting, business management, or entrepreneurship.
